About us:

Evening Land is an independent, non-partisan European newswebsite.

Founded in Amsterdam in 2022, we bring geo-political news from Europe and the world.

Articles and photos are sent to us by journalists and photographers worldwide.

In each article an expert is interviewed about his field of expertise.

Topics can be:

  • Politics
  • Economy
  • Social Affairs
  • Military
  • Space
  • Intelligence
